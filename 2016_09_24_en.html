On Call

On Call, the Montreal-based callers' collective, consists of Guillaume Bourque, Leah Dolgoy, Marie-Michèle Fournier, Sean McCutcheon, and their friends.

By hosting, and calling at, our monthly dance at the House of Friendship, we hone our skills and help build a community of local musicians, callers, and enthusiastic contradancers.
